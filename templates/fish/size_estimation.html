How It Works

Our AI-powered size estimation tool uses computer vision to:

  • Detect individual fish in pond images
  • Calculate length and estimated weight
  • Analyze size distribution across the pond
  • Determine market readiness percentage
Tips for Best Results
  • Take photos in good lighting conditions
  • Capture images from directly above the pond
  • Include a calibration object if possible
  • Ensure water clarity for better visibility
  • Take multiple images for more accurate averages
